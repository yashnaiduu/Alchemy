About Spirit Shelf

Your personal digital bar assistant, designed to help you discover, track, and master the art of mixology.

lightbulb

Our Mission

We believe that everyone should be able to make great cocktails. Whether on a budget or with a fully stocked bar, Spirit Shelf helps you make the most of what you have. No gatekeeping, just great drinks.

The Project

Built with modern web technologies like Angular 19 and PrimeNG, Spirit Shelf is a demonstration of cleaner, faster, and more beautiful web experiences. Open source and community driven.
